Augusta Praetoria (modern Aosta) was a settlement of the Salassi conquered by Rome in 25 BC. From 24 BC a colony of 3,000 Roman military veterans occupied the city of Augusta Praetoria Salassorum and it became the capital of the province Alpes Graies from 11 BC.
