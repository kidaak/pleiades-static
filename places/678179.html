An ancient settlement in Lebanon's Beqaa Valley (modern Baalbek; BAtlas 69 D1 Heliopolis/Col. Iulia Augusta Felix). A UNESCO World Heritage Site because "with its colossal structures, [it] is one of the finest examples of Imperial Roman architecture at its apogee."
