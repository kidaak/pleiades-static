Napoca (modern Cluj-Napoca) was an ancient Roman settlement established after the conquest of Dacia in AD 106. It became a provincial capital of Dacia Porolissensis and was abandoned in the late third century. Its name appears on the Roman milestone found in nearby Aiton.
