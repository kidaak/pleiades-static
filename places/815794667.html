Mikro Kameni is a non-extant island that was formed in the Santorini caldera some time before 1851, when it was attested in the Admiralty Chart of Robert Graves, HMS Volgae. It was joined to Nea Kameni at some point after this, probably in the major period of volcanic activity in 1866-1870.
